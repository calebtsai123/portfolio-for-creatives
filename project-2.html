Good:Morning

This project was done in mentorship of the amazing Alex Bakker.

Good:Morning is a fictional brand that offers physical alarm clocks designed to provide an enhanced waking experience. Recognizing the pitfalls of phones and abrupt alarm sounds, their alarm clocks provide a unique solution by incorporating a lamp that mimics the gentle rise of the sun.

In order to shift peoples behavior into how they start their day. We set out to create a sophisticated identity that encouraged dialogue focused on mornings. We found it was interesting to develop a simple colon that’s in a traditional alarm clock—but what if it took on a bigger meaning and became the hero of a design system?
