Entomix

Crickets are one of the most sustainable and protein and nutrient dense foods in the world. Although it is a norm to eat bugs around the world, westerners are still uncomfortable eating crickets. Entomix aims to be an inviting start to including bugs in their diet with their cricket-based flour that can be added to augment everyday recipes.
